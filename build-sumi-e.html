Case A · Deep Build · 深拆案例 A

一步步做出「中軸律动」Build the Sumi-e Visualizer — one prompt at a time

This is the full teardown of Case A — the audio-reactive ink-wash page. We rebuild it from a single blank sheet of rice paper into the finished piece in 11 steps, and every step is just one plain-English sentence you paste into Gemini. You never write code; you describe, you run, you check, you say the next sentence.

这是案例 A——那张随声音作画的水墨页面——的完整拆解。我们从一张空白宣纸开始，用 11 步把它做成成片，而每一步都只是你粘进 Gemini 的一句大白话。你不写代码；你描述、你运行、你检查，然后说下一句。

动手前 · 三样东西Before you start — three things

  • 一个 Gemini — 打开 Google AI Studio 或 Gemini App 的 Canvas，登录即可。Open Gemini Canvas (AI Studio or the Gemini app) and sign in.
  • 用 localhost 打开 — 麦克风、摄像头在 file:// 会被浏览器拦掉；课前用 python3 -m http.server 起一个本地服务器，再开 localhost。Mic and webcam are blocked on file:// — serve with python3 -m http.server and open localhost.
  • 一段音乐（可选） — 准备好你 Day 5 的 music-final.mp3；没有也行，第 11 步会内置一段禅意合成器，页面自己会响。An mp3 (optional) — your Day-5 music-final.mp3; or rely on the built-in synth from Step 11.

每一步都跑同一个循环：描述 → 预览 → 验证 → 迭代。每次跑通，先存一份再往下改——「上一个能跑的版本」是你最好的安全网。

Every step runs the same loop: describe → preview → verify → iterate. Save each working version before you change it — your last working copy is your safety net.

11 步阶梯The 11-step ladder — blank paper to finished piece

Step 1 builds the whole shell; Steps 2–11 are all said into the same Gemini chat — each one adds one capability on top of the last. 第 1 步搭好骨架，第 2–11 步都在同一个对话里继续说，每句加一样新能力。

1

宣纸与第一道墨环Rice paper + one brush ring

目标 — 一张暖色宣纸，正中一道手绘感的墨环。先证明「一句话变出一个页面」。Goal — a warm rice-paper canvas with one hand-brushed ink ring in the centre. Prove that one sentence becomes a page.

Prompt · 复制粘贴给 Gemini 
Build a single self-contained HTML file — one full-screen canvas,
vanilla JavaScript, no libraries. Fill it with a warm rice-paper
cream colour (#FAF6F0). In the exact centre, draw one soft black
ink-wash ring: a circle whose edge is slightly irregular and
wobbly, like a single brush stroke, not a perfect geometric circle.
Leave lots of calm empty space around it. Make it elegant and minimal.
✓ 验证 Verify
  • 正中一道墨环，边缘像毛笔画的、不是完美圆。A centred ring with a hand-drawn, imperfect edge.
  • 底色是暖米白，不是纯白。Background is warm cream, not pure white.
底层揭秘 Under the hood
沿圆周加一点正弦扰动，半径不再恒定，边缘就「毛」了。A little sine wobble added around the circumference makes the radius vary — that is the brushy edge (≈100 segments).
2

让它呼吸 + 墨痕拖尾Make it breathe + silky ink trails

目标 — 墨环缓缓起伏；画面不每帧清空，让运动留下渐渐渗进纸里的拖尾。Goal — the ring breathes slowly; the canvas is not fully cleared each frame, so motion leaves trails that soak into the paper.

Prompt · 同一个对话，继续说 
Now animate it, in the same file. Make the ring breathe forever —
its radius and edge gently wobble and pulse, slowly. And here is the
key trick: do NOT clear the whole canvas each frame. Instead, paint a
near-transparent rice-paper-coloured rectangle over everything each
frame (about 8% opacity), so moving ink leaves soft silky trails that
fade into the paper. If the visitor has prefers-reduced-motion turned
on, calm the motion right down.
✓ 验证 Verify
  • 墨环慢慢起伏，像在呼吸。The ring slowly pulses, as if breathing.
  • 运动留下会淡去的墨痕，而不是干净清空。Motion leaves fading trails, not a clean wipe.
底层揭秘 Under the hood
「拖尾」= 每帧铺一层 8% 的纸色，旧画面被慢慢盖住而非抹掉。The trail is a per-frame fillRect of paper colour at ~8% alpha — old frames fade instead of being erased.
3

放进音乐Load the music

目标 — 一个播放/暂停键 + 选本地 mp3，用 Web Audio 读出声音的频率。安全键从第一刻就在。Goal — a play/pause button + a local-mp3 picker; read the sound with the Web Audio API. Safety controls present from minute one.

Prompt · 同一个对话，继续说 
Add audio. Put a small, tasteful play/pause button and a "choose a
local audio file" picker at the bottom. Load the chosen mp3 into an
audio element and read it with the Web Audio API — create an
AnalyserNode with fftSize 256, so we can read the sound's frequencies
every frame. Keep a visible Pause and a Mute control at all times,
and never auto-blast sound.
✓ 验证 Verify
  • 选一段 mp3，能播；暂停键真的能停。Pick an mp3 — it plays; Pause actually stops it.
  • 暂停键真的能停；并按 prompt 要求保留「静音」键。Pause actually stops it; keep a Mute control too, as the prompt asks.
底层揭秘 Under the hood
AnalyserNode 是「听诊器」,fftSize 256 把声音切成 128 个频率格子供每帧读取。The AnalyserNode is the stethoscope; fftSize 256 gives 128 frequency bins to read each frame.
4

随低音起伏Make the ring follow the bass

目标 — 把墨环接到声音上：低音越重，环越大越粗；安静时收住。这是整个页面的「核心耦合」。Goal — couple ring to sound: heavy bass swells and thickens it, quiet calms it. This coupling is the heart of the page.

Prompt · 同一个对话，继续说 
Now couple the ring to the music. Every frame, read the
low-frequency (bass) energy from the analyser — just the lowest few
frequency bins — and smooth it over time. Drive the ring's size and
line thickness with that bass value: loud bass makes the ring swell
and thicken; in quiet passages it settles and calms. Keep the motion
organic, not jumpy.
✓ 验证 Verify
  • 低音一来，环就胀大变粗。On the bass, the ring swells and thickens.
  • 安静段落它平稳下来，不抖。Quiet parts settle smoothly, no jitter.
底层揭秘 Under the hood
只取最低 3 个频格当「低音」，再做平滑（lerp 0.15）避免突跳。Only the lowest ~3 bins are read as "bass", then smoothed (lerp ≈0.15) so it glides instead of snapping.
5

重拍泼墨Splash ink on the beat

目标 — 检测强低音（自适应阈值，会自调），每一拍泼出一团墨点：飞出、变慢、扩散、渗进纸里。Goal — detect strong bass with an adaptive threshold; each hit bursts ink droplets that fly, slow, spread and dry into the paper.

Prompt · 同一个对话，继续说 
Add splashing ink on the beat. Detect strong bass hits with an
ADAPTIVE threshold — after each hit raise the bar, then let it slowly
decay — so it self-tunes to any track instead of using a fixed number.
On each detected hit, burst about 30 ink droplets outward from the
centre: they fly out, slow down with friction, and as they slow they
spread bigger and dry/fade into the paper over roughly one second.
Make most droplets black ink, a few jade or bronze, a rare pale gold.
Cap it with a short cooldown of a few frames so it can't fire on
every single frame.
✓ 验证 Verify
  • 强拍泼墨，墨点扩散后干进纸里。Strong beats throw ink that spreads, then dries in.
  • 安静处不乱泼；绝不频闪。Quiet parts stay calm; it never strobes.
底层揭秘 Under the hood
自适应阈值 + 冷却帧，让它跟着歌自调、又不会狂泼；墨点变慢时才「长大变淡」=渗墨。Adaptive threshold + a short cooldown self-tune the firing; droplets grow & fade only as they slow — that is the diffusion.
6

三层墨环 + 鼠标视差Three rings + mouse parallax

目标 — 把一道环变三层（外淡、中实、内锐），跟着鼠标以不同速度位移，做出景深。Goal — one ring becomes three depth layers that follow the mouse at different speeds for parallax depth.

Prompt · 同一个对话，继续说 
Make it feel deep. Use THREE concentric ink rings at different sizes
and opacities — a faint big outer wash, a strong middle stroke, and a
small sharp inner accent. Then follow the mouse: shift the three rings
by different amounts — the inner ring moves most, the outer least — to
create parallax depth. Add a soft lag (about a third of a second) so
they drift smoothly toward the cursor rather than snapping to it.
✓ 验证 Verify
  • 看得出三层环，深浅不同。Three layered rings at different depths.
  • 移动鼠标，三层以不同速度滑动，有迟滞。Mouse moves them at different speeds, with lag.
底层揭秘 Under the hood
视差 = 同一中心、不同位移倍数（内层乘得最大）；迟滞用每帧 lerp 实现。Parallax = same centre, different offset multipliers (inner largest); the lag is a per-frame lerp toward the target.
7

冲击波扭曲Shockwave warp

目标 — 每个强拍（和点击）从中心放出一圈冲击波，所过之处把画面轻轻推开、扭曲，再淡去。Goal — each strong beat (and click) sends an expanding ring that warps everything it passes, then fades.

Prompt · 同一个对话，继续说 
Add a shockwave. On every strong beat — and whenever I click — send
an expanding ring outward from the centre that warps and distorts
everything it passes through: push points outward as the wave front
reaches them, then let it fade as it grows. Keep the distortion subtle
and elegant, like a ripple moving through wet ink.
✓ 验证 Verify
  • 强拍/点击送出一圈涟漪，墨随之弯曲。Beats/clicks send a ripple that bends the ink.
  • 扭曲克制、优雅，不刺眼。The warp is subtle, not harsh.
底层揭秘 Under the hood
所有要画的点都先过一道「位移函数」：落在波front附近就被推开，于是整幅画被同一只手扭。Every point to be drawn passes through one displacement function; points near the wave-front get pushed — so the whole image warps together.
8

拖动作画Drag to paint your own strokes

目标 — 让人能直接在宣纸上按住拖动，留下自己的墨笔触，慢慢渗开淡去。Goal — let the visitor press and drag to lay their own ink brush strokes that soak in and fade.

Prompt · 同一个对话，继续说 
Let me paint. When I press and drag on the paper — with a mouse or a
finger — lay down a trail of soft ink brush dots along my path that
wobble slightly and slowly soak in and fade, like a wet brush on rice
paper. They should take on the current ink colour. Make it work for
touch as well as mouse.
✓ 验证 Verify
  • 按住拖动，留下会渗开淡去的笔触。Drag leaves a stroke that soaks in and fades.
  • 手机触屏也能画。Works on touchscreens too.
底层揭秘 Under the hood
每个落点存进一个数组，各自带透明度逐帧衰减 + 抖动 → 自然的「湿笔」。Each touch point is stored with its own fading alpha + wobble — that gives the wet-brush feel.
9

滚轮调色 + 中轴线Scroll to recolor + the central axis

目标 — 滚轮在「青」与「铜」之间调色，整幅画一起变；再添一条贯穿上下的淡淡中轴线——画面的脊。Goal — scroll shifts the palette jade↔bronze for everything; add a faint vertical central axis line — the spine of the piece.

Prompt · 同一个对话，继续说 
Add colour control by scroll wheel. Scrolling up warms the whole
palette toward bronze; scrolling down cools it toward jade green.
Interpolate one "tone" value between cool jade and warm bronze, and
use it to colour the rings, the ink splatters, the brush strokes, and
a faint vertical "central axis" line down the middle of the page. That
axis line is the spine of the piece — the 中轴线.
✓ 验证 Verify
  • 上滚转暖（铜），下滚转冷（青），整体同步。Scroll up warms (bronze), down cools (jade), all together.
  • 中间多了一条淡淡的竖轴。A faint vertical axis line appears down the middle.
底层揭秘 Under the hood
一个 0→1 的 tone 值在青（91,140,126）与铜（176,141,87）之间插值，一处改、处处变。One 0→1 tone value lerps between jade and bronze RGB; change it once, everything recolours.
10

用你的声音 · 麦克风Your voice — the microphone

目标 — 加一个麦克风开关：你越大声，中心那团光芯越亮（颜色随当前青↔铜色调）；拍一下手（音量骤起）就炸出一团墨。Goal — a Mic toggle: your volume brightens a glowing core (in the current jade↔bronze tone); a clap (a volume spike) throws an ink explosion.

Prompt · 同一个对话，继续说 
Add a microphone toggle, labelled "Mic". When switched on, ask for
permission and read the mic with its own analyser. Let my voice volume
grow a glowing gold core in the centre — the louder I speak or sing,
the brighter and bigger it blooms. And detect a clap — a sudden spike
in volume — and make it throw an ink explosion. (Reminder: the mic
only works over localhost or https, not from a file:// page.)
✓ 验证 Verify
  • 说话/唱，中心光芯随音量发光长大。Speak/sing — the glowing core blooms with volume.
  • 拍手，炸出一团墨。在 localhost 下测。Clap → an ink burst. Test over localhost.
底层揭秘 Under the hood
「拍手」= 音量的突然跳变（这一帧比上一帧猛涨且够响）就触发爆发，而不是看绝对音量。A clap is detected as a sudden jump in volume (this frame far above the last, and loud enough), not by absolute level.
11

收尾：摄像头 · 空格钟 · 内置合成器 · 安全控件Final layer: webcam · spacebar bell · built-in synth · safety

目标 — 最后一层。让身体、键盘都能玩；没文件也自己响；并把「计分的安全规则」一次性加进去。Goal — the final layer: body and keyboard play too; it runs with no file; and the graded safety rules go in.

老实说一句 — 线上的快速示例（案例 A demo）偏重视觉，未必把静音、减弱动效都做齐；这几项是课程的计分安全要求，所以要在你自己的成片里补上，并亲自验证 Gemini 真的照做了。Honest note — the quick online example leans visual and may not implement every control (Mute, reduced-motion shake-off); these are the course's GRADED safety requirements, so add them in your own build and verify Gemini actually did.

Prompt · 同一个对话，继续说（这条最长，可分两次发） 
Final layer, four things, in the same file.
(1) Add a "Webcam" toggle: with permission, compare each camera frame
    to the previous one to find where the motion is, and use the
    horizontal position of that motion to shift the rings' parallax —
    lean or wave left and right and the ink drifts with you.
(2) Press the spacebar to "strike the bell": ring a soft synthesized
    bell tone, flash a gentle halo, and send a shockwave from the centre.
(3) If no audio file is loaded, play a built-in calm 50-BPM "zen synth"
    on its own — a soft bass kick plus occasional pentatonic chimes —
    so the page is alive the moment it opens.
(4) Safety, and it is graded: keep every flash gentle (never more than
    about three per second, never a full-screen white strobe), disable
    the screen-shake when prefers-reduced-motion is on, and keep the
    Pause and Mute controls visible the whole time.
✓ 验证 Verify
  • 对着摄像头左右动，墨随之漂移。Move left/right on webcam — the ink drifts with you.
  • 按空格，钟响、柔光一闪、冲击波散开。Space rings a bell with a soft halo + shockwave.
  • 不放文件也会自己响；闪光温和；开了「减弱动效」后抖动应停；暂停/静音键都在。Plays itself with no file; flashes gentle; with reduced-motion on the shake should stop; Pause and Mute both present.
底层揭秘 Under the hood
摄像头不做人脸识别，只把「这一帧和上一帧哪里不一样」的重心算出来当方向——便宜、稳、够用。The webcam does no face recognition — it just finds where this frame differs from the last and uses that centroid as a direction. Cheap, robust, enough.
