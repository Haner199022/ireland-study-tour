The Concept · 核心线索

钟的线索One thread runs through the whole program.

The Yongle Bell, cast around 1420, becomes a recording in a phone, becomes a verified cultural brief, becomes a track an AI composed, becomes a page strangers can scroll through. Same vibration, many media — and each AIGC module the students learn is one more way to carry it forward.
铸于 1420 年前后的永乐大钟，变成手机里的录音、可核查的文化简报、 AI 写出的音乐、陌生人能滚动的页面。同一次震动，多种媒介—— 学员学到的每个 AIGC 模块，都是把它再传递下去的一种方式。

Four Features · 四大特色

工作坊四大特色What makes this workshop different.

The 2026 "Miracle Craft Workshop" is built for international friends — to deepen knowledge of cultural relics and museums while mastering AIGC, and to add new value to traditional culture.
2026"灵境工坊"为国际友人打造：在增进文博认知的同时掌握 AIGC 应用， 为传统文博注入新的价值。

01 / Practice

沉浸式实践学习

Immersive practical learning

Say goodbye to dry theory. A "workshop" model built on hands-on practice — under mentor guidance, you experience the full creative process from concept to finished product through carefully designed projects.

02 / International

国际化课程体系

International curriculum system

Designed for learners from diverse cultures, covering text generation, music creation and video production. We focus on mainstream AIGC tools used worldwide — so your skills travel with you.

03 / Progression

从入门到精通

From beginner to expert

From mastering the core craft of "prompt alchemy" to completing a complex, comprehensive project — you can rapidly raise your AIGC application level in just one week.

04 / Community

灵感共创社群

Inspiration creation community

"Miracle Craft Workshop" builds a vibrant global community of creators. Exchange ideas with peers from around the world, spark inspiration, collaborate — and keep the momentum to learn and grow.

Day 10 · The Capstone Showcase

Capstone 展示之日A bell from 1420, music from a model, a page you can touch.

Each team presents one interlocking Capstone — Cultural Brief + AI Music + Interactive Visualizer + Prompt Log — built from the bell they recorded on Day 2. Eight minutes each, awards by dimension (not by ranking), closing ceremony, then departure.
每组展示一件互锁的 Capstone：文化简报 + AI 音乐 + 交互可视化 + Prompt 日志， 全部源自 D2 录下的那口钟。每组 8 分钟，分维度颁奖（非排名），结课仪式， 随后返程。

每组 8 分钟 + 4 分钟问答 · Per team

  • 0:00The hook — 放音乐 + 启动 visualizer，不解释
  • 1:00文化讲解 — 选了什么钟、为什么
  • 3:00AI craft — demo 最得意的一段 prompt 迭代
  • 5:00Live interaction — 邀观众操作 visualizer
  • 7:00One takeaway — 一句话带走
  • Q&A评委 + 观众提问（4 min）
