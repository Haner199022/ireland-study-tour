创作主线The Concept

创作主线The Creative Through-Line — from a prompt to a finished AI work.

Three AI modules build on one another: research a cultural theme with LLMs, turn that brief into an AI-composed music track, then make the music into an interactive visualizer you can share. Beijing supplies the raw material — Chinese painting, the Film Museum, a heritage paper craft — and the closing showcase is where each team presents the result.
三个 AI 模块层层递进：先用 LLM 做文化研究简报，再把简报变成 AI 音乐， 最后把音乐做成可分享的交互可视化。北京提供创作素材——中国画、电影博物馆、非遗手作—— 结营成果展则是每组呈现作品的舞台。

四大特色Four Features

工作坊四大特色Four Features of the Workshop — what makes it different.

Built for the University College Cork cohort — master practical AIGC tools hands-on while experiencing Beijing's culture and creative industries up close.
为爱尔兰科克大学团队打造：动手掌握实用的 AIGC 工具， 同时近距离体验北京的文化与创意产业。

01 / Practice

沉浸式实践学习

Immersive practical learning

Say goodbye to dry theory. A "workshop" model built on hands-on practice — under mentor guidance, you experience the full creative process from concept to finished product through carefully designed projects.
告别枯燥理论。以动手实践为核心的「工作坊」模式——在导师指导下，通过精心设计的项目，体验从概念到成品的完整创作过程。

02 / Modules

三大 AI 模块

Three hands-on AI modules

Text & cultural research, AI music creation, and an interactive music visualizer — taught with mainstream global AIGC tools, so the skills travel home with you.
文本与文化研究、AI 音乐创作、Gemini 音乐可视化——采用全球主流 AIGC 工具教学，技能可随你带回。

03 / Progression

从入门到精通

From beginner to expert

From mastering the core craft of "prompt alchemy" to completing a complex, comprehensive project — you can rapidly raise your AIGC application level in just one week.
从掌握「提示工程」的核心功夫，到完成一个综合性项目——在短短一周左右，快速提升你的 AIGC 应用水平。

04 / Community

灵感共创社群

Inspiration creation community

"Miracle Craft Workshop" builds a vibrant global community of creators. Exchange ideas with peers from around the world, spark inspiration, collaborate — and keep the momentum to learn and grow.
「灵境工坊」汇聚充满活力的全球创作者社群。与世界各地的伙伴交流想法、碰撞灵感、协作共创，并保持持续学习与成长的动力。

学校门面，信息与通信工程学院教学楼；楼前有捐赠的鼎与国旗。
CUC's signature building, home to the School of Information & Communication Engineering; a donated bronze tripod and the national flag stand out front.

The best study spot; a glass façade glowing at sunset, with the University History Museum and China Media Museum inside.

One of the prettiest spots, near the Library and Starlight Supermarket; a "forest oxygen bar" by day.

结营成果展Day 8 · Closing & Showcase

结营成果展Closing Showcase — each team presents its finished AI work.

On the afternoon of Day 8 (Jul 3, 14:00–16:00) each team presents the work built across the three AI modules — a verified cultural brief, an AI-composed music track, and an interactive music visualizer. Awards are given by dimension (not by ranking), with certificates and a closing ceremony — then a free final weekend.
Day 8（7 月 3 日 14:00–16:00）下午，每组展示贯穿三大 AI 模块的作品—— 文化研究简报、AI 音乐与交互可视化页面。分维度颁奖（非排名）、颁发证书与结营仪式， 随后是自由的周末。

每组 8 分钟 + 4 分钟问答 · 8-min talk + 4-min Q&A per team

  • 0:00The hook — show the work first (play the music + launch the visualizer), then explain · 先看作品（放音乐 + 启动可视化），后解释
  • 1:00创作讲解 — 选题与灵感（来自北京的素材）· Creative brief — topic & inspiration from Beijing
  • 3:00AI craft — demo your most important slice of workflow & iteration · 演示最关键的一段工作流与迭代
  • 5:00现场互动 — 邀观众体验你的交互可视化 · Live — invite the audience to explore your interactive visualizer
  • 7:00One takeaway — leave the audience with a single line · 一句话带走
  • Q&AQuestions from judges & audience (4 min) · 评委 + 观众提问（4 分钟）
