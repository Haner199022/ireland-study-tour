CUC × Irish Undergraduates · Beijing · 2026

A bell, a slider, a new voice.

一口钟·一根滑块·一个新声音

Twelve Irish undergraduates spend two weeks in Beijing — listening to a 600-year-old bell, walking the northern endpoint of an 800-year-old urban axis, sitting inside two of China's most influential AI companies, and turning all of it into music they composed with AI and a page their friends back home can touch.

十二位爱尔兰本科生用两周时间，把六百年前的钟声、八百年的中轴线、 与两家中国 AI 公司的现场，变成自己用 AI 创作的音乐， 与一张他们爱尔兰朋友能"触摸"的页面。

The Concept · 核心线索

钟的线索One thread runs through the whole tour.

A bell rung in 1420 becomes a recording in a student's phone, becomes a sound in an AI-composed track, becomes a ripple on a webpage someone in Dublin can scroll through. Same vibration, four media.
1420 年响起的一口钟，变成手机里的一段录音，变成 AI 写出的一段音乐， 变成都柏林某个人在网页上滚出的一圈涟漪。同一次震动，四种媒介。

Four Pillars · 四支柱

四块基石What the program is built on.

Four interlocking modules. Field visits feed the AI workshop. The AI workshop feeds the Capstone. Nothing is decoration.
四块互锁的模块：现场素材喂工作坊，工作坊喂毕业作品。每一块都对下游有用。

01 / Field

钟主题一日游

The Bell-Theme Day

D7. Morning at Dazhongsi (the 46.5-ton Yongle Bell, the *one bell, two tones* phenomenon). Afternoon at the Bell-and-Drum Towers — the UNESCO-listed northern endpoint of Beijing's Central Axis.

  • Record bell & drum sounds (≥ 30 s each)
  • 4-part task card: Record / Observe / Distill / Connect
  • Feeds Day 1 LLM research next morning
02 / Industry

AI 企业参观

Baidu + Xiaomi

Two contrasting visits. Baidu (D3) for generative AI — Wenxin LLM, Xiling digital humans. Xiaomi (D5) for embodied AI — HyperOS, smart home, SU7. Content vs interaction.

  • Bilingual task cards per company
  • Wenxin joins the Day-1 LLM comparison
  • Xiling digital human can narrate a Capstone
03 / Studio

3 天 AI 课程

Three days, one pipeline

Each day's output is the next day's input. Text becomes music; music becomes an interactive page. No prior coding or music training required.

  • D8 · Text: 5-exercise Prompt Gym
  • D9 · Audio: 60–120 s AI composition using real bell samples
  • D10 · Visual: Gemini-built interactive visualizer
04 / Capstone

毕业作品 + Showcase

A single interlocking work

Each team submits one integrated piece: Cultural Brief + AI Music + Interactive Visualizer + Prompt Log. Graded across four dimensions: depth, craft, communication, surprise.

  • Public showcase on D12
  • Awards by dimension (no single winner)
  • Anti-pattern red lines apply

D12 · The Public Night

Showcase 之夜What happens on Day 12.

Four teams. Eight minutes each. A bell from 1420, music from a model, a page you can touch. Awards by dimension, not by ranking. Then dinner.
四组学员 · 每组 8 分钟展示 + 4 分钟问答 · 五项分维度奖项 · 然后是晚宴。
