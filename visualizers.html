音乐可视化示例Turning sound into moving image — three interactive demos.

Three interactive pieces from the AIGC training camp: a sumi-e canvas that paints itself to sound, and two takes on a playable 3D bianzhong. Click fullscreen for the real experience. 来自 AIGC 训练营的三件交互作品：随声音作画的水墨画布，以及两种可演奏的 3D 编钟。点击全屏获得完整体验。

EXAMPLE 01 · 多模态 Multimodal

中軸律动Sumi-e Multimodal Art Visualizer

An ink-wash canvas that paints itself to sound. Speak or play into your microphone — or upload an audio file — and drag on the "rice paper" to add living brush strokes. 随声音自我作画的水墨画布。对着麦克风说话/播放，或上传一段音频；在「宣纸」上拖动即可留下灵动的笔触。

🎤 麦克风 / 上传 Mic / Upload 🖌️ 水墨 Sumi-e 🎚️ Web Audio 📷 摄像头 Camera
⛶ 全屏打开 Open fullscreen
🔊 需要声音输入：点击页面内的「麦克风接入」授权，或上传音频文件。
Needs a sound source: allow the mic inside the demo, or upload an audio file. (On a local file:// preview the mic may be blocked by the browser — use upload, or the deployed site over https.)
EXAMPLE 02 · 三维交互 3D Interactive

古韻青銅Bianzhong 3D Interactive Chime Bells

A 3D bianzhong (ancient Chinese chime bells) you can orbit, zoom and play — click the bells to ring synthesized tones, or view it in your room with AR. 可旋转、缩放、演奏的 3D 编钟：点击钟体发出合成音色，也可用 AR 置入实景。

🔔 3D model-viewer 🕶️ AR 🎚️ Web Audio
⛶ 全屏打开 Open fullscreen
🧊 3D 模型：把 Bianzhong.glb 放到 website/models/ 并部署后即显示真实模型；在此之前会显示内置的水墨矢量挂架降级图。
3D model: drop Bianzhong.glb into website/models/ and deploy to show the real model — until then the demo shows its built-in vector fallback.
EXAMPLE 03 · 可演奏 + 制作流程 Playable + How-it's-made

编钟 · 可交互 3DBianzhong — playable bells & the Gemini pipeline

A second take on the 3D bianzhong: tap anywhere on the bells to ring a tuned note — pitch follows where you click (left→right = low→high), pentatonic 宫商角徵羽. It also documents the build — how to host the model, plus a ready-to-copy Gemini prompt. 3D 编钟的另一种做法：点击钟体任意位置即发声，音高随点击位置从左(低)到右(高)，五声音阶宫商角徵羽。页面还附带制作流程——如何托管模型，以及可直接复制的 Gemini 提示词。
