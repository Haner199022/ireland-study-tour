A welcome from the School of International Communication国际传播学院的欢迎之辞

It is my privilege to welcome you to Beijing.能在北京迎接诸位，是我莫大的荣幸。

The Communication University of China has been engaged in the study of communication — its theories, its practices, and its global circulation — for more than half a century. Your presence here, as undergraduates from Ireland, is part of a longer conversation between our institutions and traditions. We do not take that lightly. 中国传媒大学投身传播研究——其理论、其实践、其全球流通——已逾半个世纪。诸位作为来自爱尔兰的本科学子莅临于此，正是我们两所学府、两种传统之间一场更为悠长的对话的一部分。对此，我们不敢轻忽。

I would also like to acknowledge our partner colleagues in Ireland, whose work has made your journey here possible. 我亦要向我们在爱尔兰的合作同仁致意，正是他们的辛劳，方使诸位的此次远行成为可能。

On what this hour is关于这一小时的意义

Not a lecture in the traditional sense.
An orientation.这并非传统意义上的讲授，
而是一次引路。

I am not going to ask you to take notes on a body of theory. I am going to ask you to consider a few questions — questions that are, in fact, unsettled in our field, and that you will encounter, in concrete form, almost every day of the next ten. 我不会要求诸位记下成套的理论，而是请诸位思索几个问题——这些问题在我们的学科之中实则尚无定论，而在未来的十天里，诸位几乎每日都将以具体的形态与之相逢。

The Question of Our Time我们时代之问

How does meaning travel
across borders today? 在今日，意义如何跨越疆界而行？

This was the question of our field fifty years ago.
The answer has changed beyond recognition. 五十年前，这是我们学科之问；
而今，答案已面目全非。

A Brief Intellectual Map一幅简略的思想图谱

The field has moved through paradigms.这门学科，已历经数次范式的更迭。

The press and broadcasting era. The cultural-imperialism debate of Schiller and his interlocutors. The turn to globalisation and flow. The platform era — what Castells called the Network Society. 报刊与广播的时代；席勒及其论敌关于「文化帝国主义」的争辩；转向全球化与流动的时代；以及平台的时代——卡斯特所称的「网络社会」。

And now, what I would suggest we call an emerging fourth paradigm: the era of synthetic media, in which the production of meaning itself is increasingly automated. 而如今，我愿称之为正在浮现的第四种范式：合成媒体的时代——在其中，意义的生产本身正日益走向自动化。

Three Structural Forces of Our Decade这十年的三股结构性力量

Three forces distinguish
the present moment.有三股力量，标定了当下这一时刻。

I · The Platform其一 · 平台

Enclosure圈占

Communication today happens largely inside proprietary digital environments.今日之传播，大多发生于私有的数字环境之内。

II · The Algorithm其二 · 算法

Computation计算

Distribution is no longer primarily editorial. It is computational.分发已不再主要由编辑主导，而是由计算主导。

III · The Model其三 · 模型

Generation生成

The act of producing text, image, voice, is now within the reach of large-scale AI systems.生产文字、图像与声音之举，如今已为大规模人工智能系统所能及。

China's Distinctive Trajectory中国独特的发展轨迹

China has lived through these transitions
in a particular sequence.中国经历这些变迁，遵循着一种独特的次第。

Many other societies passed through several decades of personal-computer-based media before encountering the mobile internet. China largely arrived at mobility first. The platform turn here is more deeply integrated with daily life — commerce, services, identity, communication — than in most other parts of the world. And the development of indigenous artificial intelligence capacity has, in the past three years, been remarkably rapid. 许多其他社会，在迎来移动互联网之前，先经历了数十年以个人电脑为基础的媒介时代；而中国大体上是径直步入了移动时代。此地的平台转向，与日常生活——商业、服务、身份、传播——的交融之深，胜于世界上多数地方。而本土人工智能能力的发展，在过去三年间更是迅猛得令人瞩目。

You will see this every day on the streets of Beijing. I would invite you to observe it not as foreign, but as instructive. 在北京的街头，诸位每日都将目睹这一切。我恳请诸位，不要将它视作陌生之物，而要视作可资借鉴的启示。

A Genuine Threshold一道真正的门槛

For most of human history,
the production of meaning
required time, training, and skill.在人类历史的绝大部分时间里，
意义的生产，须仰赖时间、训练与技艺。

A written article. A translated text. A composed image. A recorded performance. Each of these required human craft. 一篇文章，一段译文，一幅构图，一场录制的表演——其中每一样，都曾需要人的匠心。

Today, increasingly, it does not. This is not merely a tool update. It is a structural change in the economy of meaning-making. 而今日，情形正日益不再如此。这不只是一次工具的更新，而是意义生产之经济结构的一场深层变革。

A Measurable Change · The cost of translating one page of English into Chinese一项可度量的变化 · 将一页英文译为中文的成本

A fall of three orders of magnitude
in ten years.十年之间，下降了三个数量级。

2015€50.00a human translator一位人类译者
2020€5.00a junior with machine assistance一位借助机器的初级译员
2025€0.05a single model call一次模型调用

This is not a small change. It does not merely reduce expense. It reorganises who can communicate, at what scale, across which borders, and with what professional structures around the act. 这绝非微末之变。它所改变的，不只是开销，更是重新厘定了：谁能传播、以何等规模、跨越哪些疆界，又凭借怎样的专业体系来承载这一行为。

The Central Question of Our Decade这十年的核心之问

If the production cost of cultural and
communicative work approaches zero,
what cultural work becomes worth doing? 倘若文化与传播之作的生产成本趋近于零，
那么，何种文化工作方才值得去做？

I would invite you to hold this question through the next ten days. 我恳请诸位，将这一问题贯穿于未来的十日之中。

Chinese Culture Across Time穿越时间的中华文化

For a thousand years,
Chinese culture travelled slowly.千年以来，中华文化总是缓缓而行。

The silk roads. Religious missions. Scholarly translation. Library collection. Museum loan. 丝绸之路，宗教传布，学者译述，典籍庋藏，馆际借展。

Each transmission required custodianship — a person, an institution, a tradition that took responsibility for what was being conveyed and how. 每一次传递，皆须有守护之责——一个人、一所机构、一脉传统，为所传何物、如何而传而担当其责。

The New Conditions of Cultural Circulation文化流通的新境况

Today, that same culture can be circulated
in a sixty-second video.而今，同样的文化，可在一段六十秒的短视频里流转。

The constraint has shifted. The question is no longer whether culture can travel — clearly, it can. The question is what is worth sending, how it should be framed, who curates, who interprets, and to whom the work answers. 约束已然位移。问题不再是文化能否传播——显然，它能；问题在于：何者值得传送，应如何取景立意，由谁来策展，由谁来阐释，而这作品，又当向谁负责。

These are not technical questions. They are questions of communicative ethics, of responsibility, and of craft. They are the questions of our field. 这些都不是技术问题。它们关乎传播的伦理、责任与匠艺。它们，正是我们这门学科之问。

A Provocation一则诘问
Consider three objects you will encounter in the next ten days:

A six-hundred-year-old hall in the Forbidden City.
A large language model capable of processing tens of trillions of tokens daily.
A translation that costs five cents to produce.

What does international communication look like
when these three artefacts share a single room? 且思未来十日里诸位将遇见的三样事物：紫禁城中一座六百年的殿宇；一具日处理逾数十万亿词元的大语言模型；一份成本仅五分钱的译文。当这三样造物同处一室，国际传播又会是怎样的图景？

I do not have a complete answer. Neither, I believe, does our field. Your work over the next ten days is, in part, an attempted small reply. 我并无圆满的答案；我相信，我们这门学科亦无。诸位未来十日的功课，某种程度上，正是一份小小的、试探性的回应。

What You Will Create诸位将要创造之物

A single coherent work
in three elements.一件浑然一体的作品，
由三个部分构成。

I.

A research brief一份研究纲要

Written in conversation with artificial intelligence — not by it. The text is yours; the inquiry is shared.在与人工智能的对话中写就——而非由它代笔。文字属于诸位，探究则是共担的。

II.

An original composition一首原创乐曲

Together, one coherent act of cultural communication — one that you have authored. 三者相合，便是一桩浑然一体的文化传播之举——而它，出自诸位之手。

Three Observations to Carry, Daily每日随身携带的三重观照

In every setting you enter,
ask three questions.每入一处情境，
都请自问三个问题。

I.
Whose voice is being amplified?
Whose voice is being filtered, or absent?谁的声音被放大？谁的声音被过滤，或干脆缺席？
II.
What is being produced — and, by the same act,
what is being lost or displaced?何物被生产出来——而同一行为之下，又有何物被失落或被取代？
III.
Who is the audience,
and what is being asked of them?受众是谁，而又有何事被要求于他们？

These are not new questions in our field. But artificial intelligence has given them new urgency. 在我们学科里，这些并非新问题。但人工智能，赋予了它们新的紧迫。
