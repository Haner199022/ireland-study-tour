The Three Core AI Modules · 三大 AI 模块 · 围绕一口钟

A bell becomes a 3D model, a sound, an interaction — and a thing you hold.

三维IP→音乐→交互

Three modules, one chain, one bell. Day 1 turns the bronze bell into an faithful 3D model of a single chime bell and a full cultural-creative merch set you can print and hold; Day 2 gives that bell a theme song with AI music, anchored by the real recording; Day 3 turns it into an interactive page — built by describing it to Gemini — that loads the 3D bell and the music. Each module's output is the next one's raw material.

三个模块、一条线索、一口钟。Day 1 把一只青铜钟数字复刻成一个 3D 编钟（单钟）、并做成一整套文创周边（可打印、可拿在手里）； Day 2 用 AI 音乐给编钟配一首主题曲、用真实钟声锚定；Day 3 用自然语言对 Gemini 描述， 做成载入 3D 编钟与音乐的可交互页面。每个模块的产出，都是下一个模块的原料。

Day 1 · 3D & 文创 · 三维编钟与文创周边

三维编钟与文创周边3D Bianzhong & a Cultural-Creative Merch Set — from a bell to a shelf of products

The chain starts with a thing you can hold. Students digitally recreate a single bronze chime bell (one bell, not the full rack) as a real 3D model with text/image-to-3D AI (no modelling skills assumed), render a turntable, then design a complete 文创 (cultural-creative) merch set around it — a desk ornament, a blind box, a fridge magnet, a bookmark and more — and 3D-print one piece. The exported model is handed to Day 3, where the bell spins and reacts to the team's music.

Exercise 1

Know the Bell先把编钟看清楚

15 min

目标 — 复刻前先认识形制。不碰 AI：挑一只真实编钟（单钟），记录关键特征（钮 · 枚 · 鼓部 · 纹饰 · 铭文 · 青铜锈色），收集多角度参考图；写一句「文创定位」（给谁 · 什么场景 · 什么调性）。Goal — Study the form before modelling. No AI yet: pick one real bell (a single bell), note its key features (knob, studs, body, ornament, inscription, bronze patina), gather multi-angle references, and write a one-line merch positioning (for whom · what setting · what tone).

Exercise 1

Listen Like an AI像 AI 一样听歌

15 min

目标 — 学会反向拆解一首歌为 AI 可消化的 prompt 元素。教师播 3 段参考音乐（30s/段，各 2 遍），学员仅听、禁止搜歌名。Goal — Learn to reverse-engineer a track into AI-digestible prompt elements. The teacher plays 3 references (30s each, twice); just listen — no searching for titles.

参考 1Ref 1编钟独奏（如曾侯乙编钟复刻演奏）Solo bianzhong (e.g. a Marquis Yi replica performance)
参考 2Ref 2古筝 + 笛子的现代国风（如《典籍里的中国》主题音乐）Modern guofeng: guzheng + dizi (e.g. the China in the Classics theme)
参考 3Ref 3中西融合电子East–West fusion electronic
步骤 · Steps
  1. 每段闭眼听 2 遍，禁止搜歌名。Eyes closed, listen to each twice; no searching titles.
  2. 每段填 5 列：Genre · Instruments · Mood · Tempo · One word。Fill 5 columns per clip: Genre · Instruments · Mood · Tempo · One word.
  3. 把「不喜欢的地方」翻成一个可避免的 prompt 词。Turn each "thing I dislike" into a prompt word to avoid.
  4. 用英文写下三段共同的「中国感」来源。In English, write what gives all three their shared "Chinese feel."
产出 / Deliverable
每段填 5 列表：Genre · Instruments · Mood · Tempo · One word.A 5-column table per clip: Genre · Instruments · Mood · Tempo · One word.
讨论 / Discussion
  1. 哪段最难拆？为什么？Which clip was hardest to break down? Why?
  2. 把「不喜欢」翻译成可避免的 prompt 词。Translate a "dislike" into a prompt word to avoid.
  3. 三段的「中国感」共同点，能用英文说清吗？Can you state their shared "Chinese feel" in English?
✦
教学点 — AI 不懂「中国风」这种模糊词；它懂 pentatonic + guzheng + slow + reverb。Teaching point — AI doesn't understand a vague word like "Chinese style"; it understands pentatonic + guzheng + slow + reverb.
Exercise 2

First Generation第一次生成

25 min

目标 — 在海绵 / Suno 做出第一段可听的中国风音乐，体会 prompt → output 的距离。用 starter 生成后，改至少 3 个变量重生成，共 4 版。Goal — Make a first listenable Chinese-style piece in 海绵 / Suno and feel the gap between prompt and output. After the starter, change ≥3 variables and regenerate — 4 versions total.

起始 prompt · Starter · 海绵 / Suno
A 30-second instrumental piece.
- Genre: traditional Chinese instrumental, slow tempo
- Instruments: solo guzheng, faint dizi flute in background
- Mood: contemplative, vast, slightly melancholic
- Tempo: 60 BPM
- No vocals
步骤 · Steps
  1. 把 starter prompt 原样贴进海绵 / Suno，生成 v1。Paste the starter prompt as-is into 海绵 / Suno; generate v1.
  2. 每次只改 1 个变量（乐器 / 速度 / 情绪 / 混响），各重生成一版。Change one variable at a time (instrument / tempo / mood / reverb); regenerate each.
  3. 共 4 版，记调参日志：改了什么 · 听感变化。4 versions; log each change and how it sounded different.
  4. 选出最接近想象的一版留用。Keep the version closest to what you imagined.
产出 / Deliverable
4 个音频 + 调参日志（改了什么 / 听感变化 / 喜欢哪点）。4 audio clips + a tuning log (what changed / how it sounded / what you liked).
讨论 / Discussion
  1. 哪个参数改动让你「啊？！」？Which change made you go "whoa"?
  2. AI 给的 v1 与你想象差多远？How far was v1 from what you imagined?
  3. prompt 在哪被 AI 误解了？Where did the AI misread your prompt?
✦
教学点 — 音乐 prompt 是预算：你写多少细节，AI 就帮你定多少；不写则随机。Teaching point — A music prompt is a budget: the detail you write is what AI fixes for you; leave it out and it goes random.

Exercise 5

Critique & Polish互评与定稿

30 min

目标 — 用同学的耳朵当镜子做最后打磨。两轮听：盲听（不看作品名，写 3 句反馈）→ 带 brief 听（先 30 秒讲选题再放）。Goal — Use classmates' ears as a mirror for a final polish. Two listens: blind (no title, write 3 lines of feedback) → with a brief (a 30s pitch first, then play).

反馈卡 · Feedback card — 每位听众填一张/组 (one per listener/team)
听完盲听版后第一感受：______（1 句）
2 个最强时刻（时间戳）：____ / ____
1 个最弱时刻（时间戳）：____
听完 brief 后，你的感受变了吗？怎么变的？
一句话建议：______
步骤 · Steps
  1. 盲听：不报名字，每位听众写 3 句反馈（首感 + 2 强点 + 1 弱点，带时间戳）。Blind: no names; each listener writes 3 lines (first impression + 2 strong + 1 weak moment, with timestamps).
  2. 带 brief 听：先 30 秒讲选题再放，记录感受变化。With a brief: 30s pitch first, then play; note how the impression shifts.
  3. 据反馈做一次定稿（改音或改结构，二选一为主）。Make one final cut from the feedback (mainly either the sound or the structure).
  4. 覆盖 Ex.4，回收 Critique Notes。Overwrite Ex.4; collect the Critique Notes.
产出 / Deliverable
定稿音频（覆盖 Ex.4）+ Critique Notes 回收。A final audio (overwriting Ex.4) + collected Critique Notes.
讨论 / Discussion
  1. 盲听 vs 听 brief 差别最大的一组说明了什么？The biggest blind-vs-brief gap — what does it tell you?
  2. 最有用 / 最没用的反馈各是什么？Most useful vs least useful feedback?
  3. 你的 polish 改了音还是改了结构？Did your polish change the sound or the structure?
✦
收尾 · 埋点 — 每人一句话上交：「如果再加一种声音，我会加 ___。」这个「再加」就是 Day 3 可视化交互的起点。Wrap-up · seed — Each person submits one line: "If I added one more sound, it'd be ___." That "one more" is the starting point for the Day-3 interactive.

无障碍与安全 · Accessibility & Safety（先读 · read first）

本课默认有闪光、抖动、强对比脉冲——对光敏性癫痫是真实风险。下列约束全程适用并计入评分，括号内英文句可直接粘进 prompt。Flashing/shake/high-contrast pulses are a real photosensitivity risk; these apply all day, are graded, and the English lines paste straight into prompts.

防闪光 Anti-flashCap any flash to under 3 per second, never a full-screen pure-white strobe; keep flashes local and brief.
减弱动效 Reduced motionRespect @media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce): disable screen-shake and heavy bloom, soften pulses to opacity fades.
可暂停/静音 Pause & muteAlways keep a visible Pause and Mute control; nothing auto-plays loud.
对比度 ContrastEnsure text and the red seal meet WCAG AA contrast on the #FAF6F0 paper.

拆解法 · 一步一步来 · Decompose, one step at a time

不要一次让 AI 把整个页面做出来。把大目标拆成一串「单一目的」的小步骤——一次只加一件事，加完就验证，能跑再加下一步。下面这把「拆解阶梯」就是今天五个练习的主线，照着走，不会乱。Never ask the AI for the whole page at once. Break the goal into a ladder of single-purpose steps — add one thing, verify it runs, then the next. This ladder is the spine of today's five exercises; follow it and you won't get lost.
