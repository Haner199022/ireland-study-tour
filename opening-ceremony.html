A Welcome

Fáilte — and welcome to Beijing.

You arrive with curiosity, and you'll leave with something you made — your own, from start to finish.

These ten days are built around one idea: a bell becomes a 3D model, a sound, an interaction — and a thing you hold. Everything grows from a single object of Chinese culture, the bronze chime-bell, and each day's work feeds the next. Between the workshops, the city itself is the classroom.

You do not need to be an artist, a musician, or a programmer. You need curiosity and a willingness to describe what you imagine — the AI, and our team, will help you make it real. We can't wait to see what you create.

— With warm wishes, Faculty of International Media, Communication University of China

What These Ten Days Hold

Make things. See the city. Show your work.

I.

Make, don't just watch

Three AI modules around one bell — a 3D model and cultural merchandise, an AI-composed theme, and an interactive page you build by describing it to Gemini.

II.

The city is the classroom

The 798 Art Zone, the Xiaomi auto super-factory, the Film Museum, the Forbidden City and the Great Wall — context for everything you make.

III.

Show your work

The camp closes with a showcase — each team presents one complete piece of Chinese culture × AI, all drawn from the same bell.
